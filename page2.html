Overpopulation

-A threat to life on Earth-

WATCH VIDEO

Overpopulation is an undesirable condition where the number of existing human population exceeds the carrying capacity of Earth. If one community is overpopulated not all citizens are sustaining their needs. Overpopulation is caused by number of factors. In the past fifty or so years, the growth of population has boomed and has turned into overpopulation. Many people believe that overpopulation is one of the greatest threats to the world. 

10,000 years ago, humans made up 1% of the weight of vertebrate land animals: the rest were all wild. Today, wild animals make up just 1%. The other 99% is humans, our farmed animals and our pets. Our population is growing. There are now more than 7,500,000,000 people on the planet Earth. The United Nations estimates a population of nearly 9.8bn by 2050 – 30% higher than it is today. Hagonoy is a first class urban municipality in the province of Bulacan. Based on census, the population of Hagonoy as of 2015 is 125,804. With this population we can already experience the effects of overpopulation. Squatter areas, traffic, polluted water, air and lands are the examples of it. Whenever we go in public places there are so many people that make the surroundings crowded.We as a teenagers we can see this negative effects of it so we gather our ideas on how to prevent it. There are several effects of overpopulation that can have a big impact in the whole world. The Degradation of Environment, the rise number of vehicles and industries has a bad effect in the quality of air. It means that the rise of Carbon Dioxide emissions can lead to global warming. Extinction is also a major issue on the world’s wildlife. 

The demand for land grows, so too does the destruction of natural habitats, such as forests. As we all know, the high rate of unemployment is also because of overpopulation. It gives rise to unemployment as there fewer jobs to support a large number of people. The difference between demand and supply continues to expand due to overpopulation; it raises the price of various commodities including food, shelter and healthcare. This only means that people have to pay more to survive and feed their families. 

We as a teenagers we can see possible solutions to prevent overpopulation. Everyone should have better education to have some knowledge about family planning and efficient birth control that can help women making their own reproductive choices. Premarital sex should be avoided as a part of family planning. Families that are facing a hard life and choose to have four or five children should be discouraged. Making people aware of family planning can help the growth of the population lessen. Letting them know about serious effects of overpopulation and various safe sex techniques and contraceptives methods available to avoid any unwanted pregnancy that can enlighten their minds. Mostly, the information is incomplete which results in sexually active teenagers unaware of contraceptives and embarrassed to seek information about it. It is therefore important for parents and teachers to shed their old inhibitions and make their kids or students aware of solid sex education.
